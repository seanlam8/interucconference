MAP OF BERKELEY

PARKING INFORMATION

All of the cars that attendees and scholar society execs have confirmed with us will be parking in the Dana/Durant Lot, at the intersection of Dana St and Durant Ave on the south side of the Berkeley campus. This parking lot requires a parking permit, which you will receive from us at Friday night check-in from 5-9 PM at Pauley Ballroom. If you are arriving after check-in hours, please email Florence Yuan at yuan.florence@berkeley.edu to coordinate parking permit pick-up.

Your parking permit will be valid between the hours of 5:00 PM on Friday, April 29 and 9:00 AM on Sunday, May 1; we are not responsible for parking outside of these hours. Please display the parking permit face up on the driver’s side of your car’s dashboard and park in Reserved spaces. If the permit is displayed incorrectly, we are not responsible for any citations you may receive.
