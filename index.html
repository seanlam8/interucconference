What is the Inter UC Conference?

An annual event where Regents’ scholars from across the UC campuses congregate at one campus for networking, leadership development, and team-building activities. Inter-UC Conference 2016 will be held from April 29 to May 1, 2016 on the UC Berkeley campus.

Who are Regents' Scholars?

The Regents’ Scholarship (at some campuses, the Regents’ and Chancellor’s Scholarship) is the most prestigious merit-based scholarship awarded to entering UC undergraduate students. Most UC campuses have an accompanying Regents Scholar Society (RSS) or Regents’ and Chancellor’s Scholars Association (RCSA) that provides events and services for its members.
